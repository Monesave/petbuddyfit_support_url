PetBuddy Fit Support

Welcome to the PetBuddy Fit support page! PetBuddy Fit is an app that helps you track your pet's fitness, health, and activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PetBuddy Fit?
PetBuddy Fit is a mobile app that helps pet owners monitor their pets' health and fitness. Track walks, play sessions, meals, and other activities to ensure your pet stays happy and healthy.
How do I track my pet's activity?

To track your pet's activity, simply open the app, select your pet's profile, and choose the activity type (walk, play, rest, etc.). You can then record duration, intensity, and other relevant details.

How do I set fitness goals for my pet?
You can set fitness goals in the Goals section of the app. Choose activity types, frequency, and duration based on your pet's breed, age, and health needs.
Can I track multiple pets?
Yes, you can create multiple pet profiles within the PetBuddy Fit app. Each pet can have their own personalized fitness plan and activity tracking.
What if I notice a health issue with my pet?
While PetBuddy Fit can help you monitor your pet's activity and wellness, it's not a substitute for veterinary care. If you notice any health concerns, please contact your veterinarian. You can export your pet's activity data to share with your vet if needed.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing.
First, try closing and restarting the app. If that doesn't work, try restarting your device. If the problem persists, please contact support with details about your device and what you were doing when the app crashed.
I can't log in.
Make sure you're using the correct email address and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. If you're still having trouble, contact support.
My pet's activity isn't recording properly.
Ensure your device has the necessary permissions enabled for the app (location services, etc.). If you're using a connected device, check that it's properly paired with your phone and has sufficient battery.
